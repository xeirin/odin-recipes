Potato Pillow

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes (300g)
  • 2 tbsp glutinous rice flour (all purpose flour also works)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch/ potato starch
  • 1/3 tsp salt
  • 1/3 tsp pepper
  • 4 cups cooking oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes, wash them, then cut into small chunks.
  2. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes or until soft.
  3. Mash the potatoes using a fork then mix with glutinous rice flour, cornstarch, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Knead the mashed potatoes into a dough then separate into two portions.
  5. Roll the dough into logs and cut into about one inch pieces. Using a fork, press on top of the dough pieces to make a gnocchi-like pattern.
  6. Heat up the cooking oil in a pot over medium heat for 5 minutes. Deep fry the potato pieces until they turn light golden then take out of the pot.
  7. Heat up the oil over high heat for 3 minutes. Deep fry the potato pieces again for another 30 seconds then remove from pot.
  8. Serve and enjoy the potato pillows by themselves or with chili powder or ketchup.
